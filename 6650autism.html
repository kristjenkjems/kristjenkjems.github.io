Particpatory Design Project: Autism and Philly AIMS

In conjunction with Dr. David Mendell and the Philly AIMS staff, myself and other students underwent an exploratory participatory design study focused on teacher's use of evidence based practices within special needs classrooms. This included enhancing already existing programs, developing new digital media tools, and involving parties, such as parents and principals, that were not previously involved as much as they could, or perhaps should, be.
